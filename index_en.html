Located at 45 degrees north latitude, Heilongjiang's golden corn belt, often referred to as the "cold black soil," is home to tens of thousands of acres of fresh waxy corn fields. Our Corn's organic fresh waxy texture is 100% organic and genuinely healthy, without relying on vacuum packaging or artificial additives. This commitment ensures the highest quality and delivers truly organic corn you can trust.

Star Product

Organic Yellow Waxy Corn
